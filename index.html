  • Sound and Mood

    CS 4701: Practicum in Artificial Intelligence

    August - December 2021

    How do audio features affect the mood of a song? For this project, we wanted to test our hypothesis that songs possessing similar attributes and aesthetics in terms of audio metrics will likely hold a similar emotional response. To do this, we trained a few machine learning models (K-Nearest Neighbors, Support Vector Machines, and Random Forest Classifiers) using the scikit-learn library on data scraped from Spotify's mood playlists. We evaluated the models using the remaining songs outside of our training and test data from each playlist, tweaking different variables of the algorithms, and persisted the classifier with the best accuracy.

    We used our persisted model in our web interface which allowed users to search for songs and see the mood classification, and how its audio features compare to the audio features of the songs in our training and test data. We used D3.js library for visualizing the data.

    Check out our repo for more details!
